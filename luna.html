About Our Event. It'll blow your mind.

Held towards the end of December every year, it attracts a whopping crowd of over 1,39,000 students from more than 1700 colleges nationwide. Kreiva is devoted to creating an unforgettable experience for all its visitors, simultaneously providing unique opportunities to the youth for showcasing their talents in front of extremely large audiences and top-notch judges.
